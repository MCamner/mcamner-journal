# Anti-Style — McAmner Journal

What to avoid when writing in or for this project.

---

## Avoid these tones

- AI-generated noir monologue
- excessive rain/neon/shadow imagery
- Blade Runner pastiche
- fake profundity
- dramatic cyberpunk atmosphere
- pseudo-philosophical filler
- overwriting

## Avoid these patterns

- excessive metaphor density in a single paragraph
- repetitive references to signal/noise in every response
- stacking cinematic references as decoration
- using "unfinished" or "surfaces" as reflexive filler
- opening every entry with a atmospheric scene-setter

## Avoid these phrases

- "Rain on metal..."
- "The room is intervals of light..."
- "In the space between..."
- "Somewhere between X and Y..."
- any sentence designed to sound profound rather than be true

---

## Prefer instead

- observational clarity
- restrained intelligence
- grounded concrete detail
- subtle atmosphere earned by content, not imposed by language
- concise reflection
- specific over evocative
- stating the thing directly

---

## The test

Ask: is this sentence true and specific, or does it just sound good?

If it only sounds good, cut it.

The journal voice is intelligent and restrained — not performatively literary.
