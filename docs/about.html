>> black iris

Artist-run platform founded with Anna Camner in Stockholm. Exhibitions, writing, and long-term collaborations — often in locations not normally open to the public.

The focus is context: how work is encountered, framed, and experienced, rather than scale or spectacle. Black Iris operates through collaboration rather than representation. The artists involved share an interest in process, material, and sustained inquiry.

Explosive Vision at Gasverket during Stockholm Art Week brought wide attention within the local scene. The platform continues.
