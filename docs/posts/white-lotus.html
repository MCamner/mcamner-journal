>> white lotus s3

HBO · 2025 · Mike White · Thailand

Each season moves further from spectacle. Season one had blood. Season two had opera. Season three has something harder to dispel: the particular unease of people who have everything they wanted and don't know what to do with it.

The humor is quieter now, almost embarrassed by itself. What replaces it is observation — the careful, uncomfortable kind. Characters who live entirely inside their own readings of themselves. The show watches them without correcting them. That refusal to provide moral release is increasingly the point.

Thailand is another surface for the same excavation. The hotel accommodates everything. What the guests bring with them doesn't change with the location — only the backdrop does. Status. Desire. Shame. They check in easily. They never check out.

Rating: recommended
