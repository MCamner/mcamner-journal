>> dope thief

Apple TV+ · 2025 · Brian Tyree Henry, Wagner Moura

Two friends. One idea that shouldn't have worked but almost did. And then the thing underneath what they found.

Brian Tyree Henry carries this in stillness. The best scenes are the quiet ones — two men in a car, not saying what they both know. The crime escalates because the world around them escalates. What starts as a grab becomes an exposure. What's exposed doesn't want to stay hidden.

The show earns its pace because it trusts the characters. The plot is almost incidental to the dynamic at its center — friendship as the only stable thing in a situation that keeps getting less stable.

Most crime shows are about what people do. This one is about what they owe each other. That's the harder question and the show knows it.

Rating: recommended
