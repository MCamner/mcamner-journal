>> all seeing, all blind

Black Iris · Stockholm · 2025 · Ulrika Sparre, Dit Cilinn, David Ohlsson, Matthias van Arkel, Julia Perone, Gunilla Klingberg, Annee Olofsson, Johan Thurfjell, Marcus Mårtenson, Juan-Pedro Fabra Guemberena, Anna Camner, Jesper Nyrén, Stefan Otto, Daniel Jensen

The fourth exhibition. Thirteen artists. The question the show is built around: how reliable is seeing?

All Seeing, All Blind works in the space between clarity and distortion — in what perception adds to what is there, in what memory removes from what was. The artists approach this from different directions and the overlap between them is not resolved but held in tension.

Vision as the subject means the exhibition is partly about the viewing of the exhibition itself. What you bring to a room. What the room does to it. The title doesn't resolve the contradiction — it names it.
